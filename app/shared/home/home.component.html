How It Works

Donate Now to select and customise your virtual remembrance globe in celebration of a loved one and add it to our tree. View and share your dedicated messages with friends, family and colleagues on your favourite social media platform.

Join us for the tree-lighting ceremony on the 24th of November 2019 at the Johannesburg Zoo. The special event continues to be a beacon of hope, strength and a celebration of life

The funds raised from this initiative ensure that HospiceWits continues to provide hope, comfort and support to thousands of patients and families per year who have been impacted by limiting illness.

If you would like to purchase a remembrance globe in memory of a loved one or you'd simply like to celebrate the life of someone special, please click on the Donate Now button to dedicate your globe online.
